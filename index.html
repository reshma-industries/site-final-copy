Our core products

Engineered for strength, designed for a clean finish.

Concrete M-Sand

Eco-friendly alternative to river sand. Cubical particle shape for better compressive strength.

Concrete
  • •Uniform grading for consistent mixing
  • •Improved workability and finish
  • •Ideal for RCC and concrete works

Plastering P-Sand

Finely graded for wall plastering. Helps reduce cracks and gives a smoother finish.

Plastering
  • •Fine texture for clean trowel finish
  • •Lower paint consumption with smoother walls
  • •Suitable for internal and external plastering

Smart Interlock Bricks

Self-locking bricks for faster laying and cleaner alignment. Great for efficient builds.

Interlock
  • •Reduced mortar use (project-dependent)
  • •Faster construction cycles
  • •Uniform edges and better finish

Chamber Red Bricks

Classic high-fired red clay bricks for long-lasting builds and better insulation.

Red Brick
  • •Strong, durable, time-tested
  • •Thermal comfort and sound damping
  • •Ideal for residential and boundary walls
